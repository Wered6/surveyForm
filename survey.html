The objective of the game is to capture the opponent's master, which is located on the opponent's side of the board. The game is played in turns, with each turn consisting of two phases: the movement phase and the action phase. The movement phase consists of moving one of the player's pieces to an adjacent square. Gloomhaven

Gloomhaven is a game of Euro-inspired tactical combat in a persistent world of shifting motives. Players will take on the role of a wandering adventurer with their own special set of skills and their own reasons for traveling to this dark corner of the world. Players must work together out of necessity to clear out menacing dungeons and forgotten ruins. In the process they will enhance their abilities with experience and loot, discover new locations to explore and plunder, and expand an ever-branching story fueled by the decisions they make.
